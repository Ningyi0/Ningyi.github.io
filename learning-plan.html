Learning Plan

Personal strengths and weaknesses: I suppose my strengths would be my detail oriented thinking and patience. I cannot manage multiple acitivities well at the same time, I tend to thrive more on micro-level skills and I am naturally more attentive and focused when rules are ambiguous. This has made learning very hard for me in the past because I don’t learn well in classrooms and I also don’t have the self-discipline to learn successfully on my own. Art school has been really healing for me and I think it has given me the confidence to try Web Development. I have had a lot of stressful and time-consuming things happen during the previous few sprints and it has taught me a lot about prioritising and time-management. I will try my best to make plans for my learning as well as other obligations. To list some of my current obligations, I have a part-time 16 hour job which are mostly scheduled for Fridays and Sundays now and weekends once the bootcamp begins.
